A Story
About College

I entered college with zero prior programming experience. In other words, my first semester was rough. It was a common occurrence to catch me asking a friend questions like...

"A directory is a folder..?"

"What is a package..?"

I tried to alleviate this knowledge disparity by learning from some Web Development books, which only moderately helped. I was dumbfounded by a simple issue where I could not create data on a web page that persisted after a refresh.

This issue plagued me until I found myself as a volunteer at a hackathon. A participant asked me for help fixing his database, to which I had no knowledge yet of how to help. Funny enough, I began asking him questions and taking my first steps in learning the solution to my persistent data issue.

Upon returning home, I immediately began researching databases and their innerworkings. I had found my solution, and opened a plethora of doors to countless more questions. After stepping through these doors, I found my passion for Web Development and Software Engineering.

And Now?

Presently, after some fantastic years learning and growing, I realized my love for applying logic and design to create funtional, seamlessly effective, and visually appealing works through programming.


I look forward to growing and continuing this journey!
